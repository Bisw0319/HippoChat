How to Use HippoChat?

Creating a Room

  • Click "Create New Room" on the welcome screen.
  • Enter your username (max 20 characters).
  • Choose room name (optional).
  • Set auto-delete timer (5-120 minutes)
  • Click "Create Room" to generate encrypted room.

Joining a Room

  • Click "Join Existing Room" on the welcome screen.
  • Enter the Room ID (shared by room creator).
  • Escape the "#" when enter the Room ID.
  • Enter your username (max 20 characters).
  • Click "Join Room" to enter the chat.
ProTip: Share the Room ID securely with people you want to chat with. All messages are automatically encrypted!
